Motorbikes / Quads

In the past, motorbike riders have had a raw deal. They have been offered tyre sealants that do not properly suit their machines. Now all that has changed.

OKO offers not one but Three different types of tailored motorbike tyre sealant –

1. OKO (500ml bottle) for On-Road Motorbikes, Mopeds & Scooters. Treats 2 moped/scooter tyres, or 1-2 motorbike tyres depending on size. Also for use in motorised rickshaws. Speed rated to 80mph/ 130km/h.

2. OKO (1250ml bottle) for Quad Bikes – for on and off road use – from farming: to racing! Treats 4 tyres of most quad sizes.

3. OKO XT (800ml bottle) – for the toughest Off-Road Racing Enduro & Motocross conditions. Seals punctures and slits. NO OTHER sealant comes close. Treats 2 enduro/motocross tubes or tubeless tyres. Way cheaper than mousse: much easier to fit: does not harm the ride.

They all last for the legal life of the tyre, unlike many others which need replacing after a few months. OKO instantly seals multiple punctures with minimal loss of air or sealant. Even if the hole is too big, it helps slow down the pressure loss, allowing you to stop the bike safely.

OKO helps keep tyres at their correct pressure, for safer riding & fuel saving. It is certified environmentally safe, containing rust inhibitor; it will not damage rims, wheels, tyre casings or inner tubes.

Rigorously tested in all conditions: functional in extreme heat and cold.

The OKO motorbike and quad bike tyre sealant range is suitable for use in most tyre types – tubed, tubeless, road, off-road etc., and for all conditions. Do not use in low profile tyres, tyres of V rating or faster, wheels/tyres fitted with internal TPMS (Tyre Pressure Monitoring Systems), or with chrome wheels (chromed finishes are very fragile and any chemical may damage them: OKO is safe for use with steel and alloy wheels, including polished alloys).

The sealants are easy to apply with the valve core remover and filling tube supplied in every bottle – and they are also all available in 25 litre drums with calibrated drum pumps for workshop use: so get proper puncture protection for your machine.
